About

From hands-on industries into software development.

My background is not a traditional one, and that has become a strength. I bring discipline, communication, and a practical problem-solving mindset from years of work outside tech while I build a stronger software foundation.

My story

I was born in Portland, Oregon and moved around the country growing up. I first realized I wanted to learn programming around 15 years ago while spending time in the modded Battlefield 2 community behind Project Reality, which later became Squad. That experience made software feel less abstract and more like something real people could build together.

Today I am enrolled in the College of Western Idaho software development program. I am using that program to strengthen my skills across the web stack, Python, SQL, and C# while building projects that look more professional and communicate more clearly.

Currently studying

Client-side and server-side development, database systems, collaborative software development, and cloud platform fundamentals.

Currently seeking

Internship, junior developer, or entry-level software opportunities where I can contribute, learn from a team, and keep improving through real project work.
